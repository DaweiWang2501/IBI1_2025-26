My first Git experience blended excitement and challenges. I learned core commands—git init, add, commit, push……and felt thrilled seeing my code on GitHub.
Struggles included resolving merge conflicts and fixing accidental staging. I now write clear commit messages and make small, frequent commits.

What once felt complex now feels empowering, teaching me discipline and the value of intentional work. I’m eager to explore more!
